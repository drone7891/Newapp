About

The Beeclust Multi Robot Systems Lab is a research based team started by the students of SRMIST. Beeclust lab specializes mainly in the field of swarm robotics and swarm intelligence. Currently our team is making embarkable progress in field of mobile printing using micro controlled robots that perform tasks collectively.
